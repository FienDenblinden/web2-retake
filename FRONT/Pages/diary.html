Do you have a small weather station at home and would you like to follow up all data, day by day? Or are you also so fascinated by the weather and would you like to have a clear overview? This is possible with Temperatura! Make your own diary! Write down the weather every day. Also the temperature, the humidity, ... and watch it back whenever you want. This tool makes Temperatura so unique compared to other weather sites. It is recommended to fill in this diary every day at a regular time.
